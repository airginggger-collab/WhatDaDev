01 — Промпт для Stitch

Собрать UI-kit в Stitch

Этот промпт заставляет Stitch собрать UI-kit (style guide) — один экран со свотчами цветов, типошкалой и компонентами в тёмной теме WhatDaDev, а НЕ лендинг. Вставьте его целиком в поле запроса Stitch («Describe your design» / «Опишите дизайн»). Английский работает надёжнее.

Prompt · English
Style guide screen: a single scrollable UI-kit that shows only styles and components — no hero, no marketing copy, no product or landing sections. Dark mode, no emoji. Include these labeled sections: 1. Color palette — swatches with hex captions: Background #10151A, Surface #171E26, Surface-alt #1C242E, Text #F4F2E9, Muted #99A3AE, Border #263140, Accent #1F5B99, Accent-on-dark #8FBBE0 and #BFD7EC. 2. Typography — specimen rows for H1, H2, H3, body and caption, each with its name and pixel size. 3. Buttons — primary (fill #1F5B99, white label) and secondary (outline), each shown in default and hover state. 4. Inputs — a text field and a textarea, with focus state. 5. Tags / badges plus status colors: success #16A34A, warning #D97706, danger #DC2626. 6. Cards — a default card and a featured card with a 2px #1F5B99 border. 7. Small parts — an eyebrow label and breadcrumbs. Styling tokens: background #10151A, surfaces #171E26 / #1C242E, borders 1px #263140, text #F4F2E9, muted #99A3AE. Accent blue #1F5B99 with white label; on dark use lighter #8FBBE0 / #BFD7EC for links and accents. Not indigo, not red. Headings: serif Piazzolla (fallback Georgia), weight 560. Body: Golos Text. Numbers, labels, captions, tags, buttons: IBM Plex Mono, labels uppercase with letter-spacing. Corner radius 6px for cards, 4px for buttons and tags. Rectangular — no pill shapes, no glow, no blurred shadows; only a hard 3px offset shadow on hover.
